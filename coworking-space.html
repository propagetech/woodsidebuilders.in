Woodside Coworking Space

This is an elegant, luxurious co-working space spread across 5000 sq. ft., situated in Bhashyam Circle, Sadashivnagar, one of the most sought out areas in Bengaluru. It is the ideal, secure office space to accommodate business teams of any size. The space is aptly designed with the latest infrastructure, keeping in mind the modern corporate needs. Here you have access to all the essential amenities you need to operate your business efficiently. It consists of a meeting room, 20 spacious private cabins and many workstations that can accommodate more than 100 people, all ergonomically designed.

It is one of the most preferred co-working spaces since it is cost-effective, flexible, and highly conducive for business growth. This is an energetic and dynamic space where you will be able to put undivided focus on your work and be highly productive.

Our rates are very economical, with the starting rent of work station only INR 3000 and that of the private cabins INR 5000 per month. You can get access to these spaces 24x7 and expect to receive all essential amenities like security services, or WiFi, to name a few. In addition, we provide daily cleaning and sanitation facilities too.

Our team is committed to making your workdays more fun, efficient, and productive. Whether you are a startup, freelancer, a business enthusiast, or an established organization, our centrally-located Woodside co-working space is the space you need.
